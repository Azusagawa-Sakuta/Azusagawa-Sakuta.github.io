# Data Lab
The most interesting function implemented in integer is `howManyBits`, which requires a binary/half interval technique.

Also, it's worth remembering the IEEE 754 floating point numbers standard, and special forms such as the denormalized, NaN, $\pm \text{inf}$ and $\pm 0$.
